Singer Name: George Wassouf

Basic Information:
Full Name: George Wassouf
Country: Syria
Born: December 23, 1961
Genre: Arabic pop, Tarab

Early Life:
George Wassouf began singing at a very young age and quickly gained attention due to his distinctive voice.

Career:
He moved to Lebanon, where he became widely popular. His career expanded across the Arab world with concerts and albums.

Musical Style:
- Combines traditional tarab with modern pop
- Highly emotional vocal delivery
- Strong connection with audience

Themes:
- Love and heartbreak
- Emotional struggle
- Personal expression

Famous Songs:
- Kalam El Nass
- Sabra Ya Ana
- El Hawa Sultan
- Sabrak Alaya

Impact:
He is one of the most popular contemporary Arabic singers and has influenced modern music trends.

Legacy:
Despite challenges, he remains a dominant figure in Arabic music.
