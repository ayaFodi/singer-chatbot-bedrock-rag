Singer Name: Sabah Fakhri

Basic Information:
Full Name: Sabah Abu Qaws
Country: Syria
Born: 1933
Died: 2021
Genre: Arabic classical, Tarab

Early Life:
He showed exceptional talent early and studied classical Arabic music traditions.

Career:
He became famous for preserving Aleppian music, especially muwashahat and qudud halabiya.

Performance Style:
- Extremely long performances (sometimes several hours)
- Strong vocal endurance
- Active audience interaction

Musical Features:
- Mastery of maqamat
- High improvisation skills
- Strict adherence to classical traditions

Famous Songs:
- Qadak Al Mayyas
- Ya Mal Al Sham
- Khamrat Al Hob
- Abat Al Layali

Impact:
He played a major role in preserving traditional Arabic music in the modern era.

Legacy:
He is regarded as one of the greatest classical performers in Arabic music history.
