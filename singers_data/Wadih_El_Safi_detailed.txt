Singer Name: Wadih El Safi

Basic Information:
Full Name: Wadih Francis
Country: Lebanon
Born: November 1, 1921
Died: October 11, 2013
Genre: Lebanese folk, Arabic classical

Early Life:
Wadih El Safi was born in a mountainous region of Lebanon. His surroundings influenced his powerful, natural vocal style.

Career:
He rose to fame after winning a national singing competition. He became a central figure in Lebanese music and worked to preserve its traditional roots.

Musical Style:
- Strong, deep, and resonant voice
- Inspired by mountain echo techniques
- Focus on traditional Lebanese rhythms and melodies

Themes:
- Homeland and patriotism
- Nature and mountains
- Cultural heritage

Famous Songs:
- Lebnan Ya Otaat Sama
- Ya Jabal
- Al Layl Ya Layla
- Tal El Sahr

Impact:
He helped define Lebanese musical identity and influenced many future artists.

Legacy:
He is considered one of the pillars of Arabic and Lebanese music.
